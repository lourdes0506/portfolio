PROJECTS

1. Mealshare

MealShare is a social impact platform developed to connect restaurants and event organizers with NGOs to reduce food waste and combat hunger. It allows verified donors to list surplus food and enables NGOs to claim and collect it efficiently. The system includes real-time notifications, authentication, and proof of distribution. This initiative promotes sustainable food practices while supporting underprivileged communities.

2. Avian Acoustics: Deep Learning for Bird Species Identification and Classification from Vocalizations

Avian Acoustics is an AI-powered system designed to identify and classify bird species based on their vocalizations. By using deep learning models like Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) the system analyzes audio recordings and detects bird species even in noisy environments. This solution supports researchers and conservationists in monitoring bird populations and biodiversity without direct human intervention. It offers a scalable, accurate and cost-effective method for ecological research and wildlife protection.

3. Nutrient NPK Sensing and Monitoring System in Hydroponics

Nutrient NPK Sensing and Monitoring System in Hydroponics is designed to track the levels of essential nutrients—Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K)—in a soilless farming setup. The system uses sensors to detect nutrient imbalances in real time and alerts the user for corrective action. This ensures healthy plant growth, improves yield quality, and reduces nutrient waste. It's a smart, efficient solution for sustainable and precise agriculture

4. Motion Detection By Image Processing

A computer vision-based system was developed to detect motion by analyzing differences between consecutive video frames. Using image processing techniques like background subtraction, it accurately identifies movement in real time. The system is well-suited for surveillance, smart monitoring, and automated alerts. It ensures high reliability by minimizing false detections caused by environmental noise or lighting changes. This project demonstrates the effective use of real-time image analysis for intelligent and responsive security solutions.

INTERNSHIPS

1. Hyundai Motors – Line Safety Automation

At Hyundai Motors, we developed a sensor-based safety system that integrated motion and temperature sensors to detect human presence and halt machinery when safety lines were crossed. This system enhanced worker safety by minimizing false triggers and ensuring accurate human detection. Following implementation, we collected and analyzed operational data from before and after the system was deployed. Using Microsoft Power BI, we created a comprehensive dashboard to visually compare the effectiveness and impact of the safety solution. The internship provided valuable experience in automation, sensor integration and data-driven analysis. It also highlighted the importance of safety compliance in industrial environments.

2. Southern Railways – Speaker & CCTV System

As part of the internship at Southern Railways, we designed and installed a complete speaker system for a railway-operated school, ensuring clear and effective communication across the premises. We also troubleshooted and resolved a critical issue in the CCTV surveillance system at the railway hospital, restoring its monitoring functionality. Additionally, we studied the live announcement system at Chennai Central railway station, gaining insight into how real-time audio relays are managed across platforms. The internship provided practical exposure to public infrastructure systems, technical problem-solving, and audio-visual integration. It also strengthened our understanding of safety protocols and system maintenance in government institutions.

3. Verzeo – IoT-Based Risk Assessment

During the Verzeo IoT Internship, we explored the application of Internet of Things (IoT) in real-world risk assessment scenarios. We designed a prototype system using sensors to detect and monitor environmental risks such as gas leaks and abnormal temperature levels. The system was programmed to trigger alerts in real time, helping prevent hazardous incidents. We also worked on integrating the collected sensor data with cloud platforms for continuous monitoring and remote access. This internship deepened our understanding of embedded systems, sensor networks, and IoT-based safety solutions. It strengthened our skills in combining hardware and software for smart risk management.
